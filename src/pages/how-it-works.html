The Legal Bit

Planning Permission & Building Regulations – Explained

Planning Permission

Almost never needed.

In nearly all cases your conversion falls within Permitted Development rights, meaning no formal planning application is required. We confirm this in writing during your survey.

Building Regulations

Always handled by us.

Building regs ensure structural and thermal compliance. We file all paperwork, liaise with Local Authority Building Control, and hand you the completion certificate at sign-off.

Please note: Our services are not available for listed buildings or properties in conservation areas. If in doubt, contact us – we'll let you know in minutes whether we can help.
